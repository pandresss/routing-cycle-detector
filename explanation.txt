Stream file line-by-line, group edges into adjacency lists by (claim_id, status_code). For each group, iterative DFS from every node finds the longest simple cycle back to start, tracking visited nodes via frozensets. Runs ~30s on 11M lines. Memory: O(edges). Complexity: O(N*2^N) per group but groups are small (~6 nodes). Future: parallel processing, early pruning of small groups.
